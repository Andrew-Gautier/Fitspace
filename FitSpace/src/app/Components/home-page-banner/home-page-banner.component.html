Bug Reporting

Our site is currently undergoing beta-testing. Any issues, problems, or suggestions you might have, please send to our email. If you are reporting a bug, please be descriptive about what cause it.

About

Fitspace is a content platform for sharing fitness and wellbeing information to all. Workout routines, healthy diets, and fitness questions are all things you can find on this site.

When interacting with others on fitspace, be respectful and kind to others. Everyone is here with the same goal: self improvement. Inappropriate comments or posts will be removed.

If you are interested in becoming a verified trainer, send your application to our email. Be sure to include your name, gym, and any certifications you think would help us in our decision-making process.
